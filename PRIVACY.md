# Privacy Policy for Odesa AI

**Last Updated:** January 19, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Odesa AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our AI Operating System accesses, uses, and stores your data.

## 2. Data We Access
To function as an autonomous assistant, Odesa AI requests access to:
* **Google User Data:** Email address and profile information (for authentication).
* **Gmail API:** Read-only access to analyze emails (only when requested by you) and Draft access to prepare emails.
* **Google Calendar API:** Read and Write access to manage your schedule.

## 3. How We Use Your Data
* **AI Processing:** Your data is processed by Large Language Models (LLM) providers (Google Gemini) solely to generate answers and execute tasks.
* **No Sale of Data:** We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others.
* **Limited Use:** Our use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

## 4. Data Storage
* We minimize data storage.
* Session tokens are stored securely on your local device.
* Vector memory (your preferences) is stored in our encrypted database (Supabase).

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions, please contact us at: **admin@odesa-ai.org**
